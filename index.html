Allen Computer Science

Welcome to allencs.org, the home to Allen High School's computer science clubs.

We are made up of several forums, such as the Computer Science Honor Society or Girls Who Code. Our umbrella organization has a meeting every Tuesday except the first of the month at 4:30 PM.
